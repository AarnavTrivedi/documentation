# How to join our community on Matrix

## 1. Go to [app.element.io](https://app.element.io/)

Click on the "Create account" button.

![Create account](/create-account.png)

## 2. Press "edit" to change the homeserver

![Edit homeserver button](/edit-homeserver-button.png)

## 3. Enter the homeserver URL

![Enter homeserver URL](/enter-matrix-zerodao-gg.png)

## 4. Create your account

Simply enter your username and password and press "Create account". Finish the login flow from there.

## 5. Join the community room and say hi!

Press [this link](https://matrix.to/#/#arguflow-general:matrix.zerodao.gg) to join the community room.

Once you are in, say hi! We will place you into the right rooms from there.
