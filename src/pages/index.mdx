# Trieve Documentation

Trieve is Infrastructure for building AI search into your applications.

<Note>

Trieve combines powerful language models with tools for human fine tuning. Get features like dense vector semantic search, sparse vector full-text search, cross encoder re-ranker models, RAG endpoints, relevance weighting, and more in a single service.

</Note>

Get the most out of Trieve by following our guides and using our resources.

## Things still unclear? Check out our live demos or [REST OpenAPI docs](https://api.trieve.ai/redoc)

We have public Trieve demos for for the YCombinator company directory and another for HN comments and posts coming soon!

- [YCombinator companies search](https://yc.trieve.ai)
- Search for HN comments and posts coming soon!
